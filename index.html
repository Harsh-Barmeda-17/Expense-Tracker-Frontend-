Welcome to Expense Tracker

This app will help you track your income and expenses, set budgets, and visualize your financial data.

Here's a quick guide to get you started!

Adding Transactions

1. Click on the "Add Transaction" card

2. Fill in the amount, description, date, type, and category

3. Use the toggle to set recurring transactions (like rent or subscriptions)

4. Click "Add Transaction" to save

Managing Categories

You can add your own custom categories:

1. Click the "+" button next to the category dropdown

2. Or go to the "Budgets" button and select "Manage Categories"

3. Enter a new category name and click "Add"

Setting Budgets

Set monthly budgets to track your spending:

1. Click the "Budgets" button

2. Select a category and enter the budget amount

3. Click "Add Budget"

You'll get alerts when you reach 75% or 100% of your budget!

Viewing Reports

The dashboard provides visual reports:

• Pie Chart: Shows your expense categories breakdown

• Bar Chart: Compares monthly income vs expenses

• Line Chart: Tracks your balance over time
